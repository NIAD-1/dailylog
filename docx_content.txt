INTRODUCTION:

The report provides a comprehensive overview of the activities carried out by the Post-Marketing Surveillance (PMS) Directorate, Lagos, for the month of Dec 2025. It captures details of routine surveillance across various regulated product categories, alerts received and acted upon, consumer complaints investigated, GLSI monitoring, recalls, and revenue generated. The report also highlights stakeholder engagements, capacity-building activities, as well as key challenges encountered, and recommendations proposed for improved operational efficiency.

During the previous month, the Office investigated a complaint on unapproved labelling on some seasonings (Maggi, Terra seasoning cubes) during the investigation, it was observed that the product labels had been approved in error. The activity proceeded till December during which the manufacturing facilities of Nestlé Nigeria and WACUB (TGI Group) were visited in order to take the inventory of the non-compliant label to enable the Agency make informed regulatory decision.

A report of suspicious sales of adulterated product
